Hannah Montana Is Back —
And She Never Really Left

Twenty years ago, a teenage girl with a big secret changed television forever. Her name was Miley Stewart, but the world knew her as Hannah Montana — a pop star with a blonde wig and an iconicFamous and highly admired, representing a whole era. smile. The show premieredTo be shown or performed in public for the very first time. on Disney Channel in March 2006, and millions of kids around the world fell in love with her double lifeWhen someone secretly lives two very different lives at the same time..

The idea was simple but brilliant: Miley was a normal teenager at school, but at night, she performed on stage as the famous Hannah Montana. Nobody knew her secret — not her friends, not her classmates. Only her family. This story of living a double lifeWhen someone secretly lives two very different lives at the same time. became one of the most popular TV shows in Disney history.

Now, in 2026, Miley Cyrus — the real person behind Hannah Montana — is 33 years old. And this week, she is celebrating the anniversaryThe date on which something important happened in a previous year. of the show with a very special event on Disney+. In the Hannah Montana 20th Anniversary Special, Miley goes back to the sets of the original show, performs some of her most famous songs, and reunites with her dad, Billy Ray Cyrus, who also acted in the series.

Miley also invited some very special guests. Selena Gomez made a surprise appearance, arranged by podcast host Alex Cooper. And superfansPeople who are extremely passionate and devoted to something. from all over the world — Brazil, France, Miami — flew to Los Angeles just to be in the audience. For many of them, the experience was deeply emotional.

"It was healing for my inner child."

— Axel Cazenave, superfan from Miami
Hannah Montana by the numbers
  • More than 500 million hours streamed on Disney+ this decade
  • Season 1 album was the first TV soundtrack to reach #1 on Billboard 200
  • The 2007 Best of Both Worlds tour sold out 71 arenas across North America
  • The show ran for 4 seasons from 2006 to 2011

The show's legacySomething that stays important or remembered long after it happened. is impossible to ignore. More than half a billion hours of Hannah Montana have been streamed on Disney+ in recent years. The Season 1 album was the first TV soundtrack to reach number one on the Billboard 200. And Miley's 2007 tour sold out 71 arenas across North America.

But does this mean a rebootA new version of an old TV show, movie or franchise. is coming? Probably not. When a reporter asked Miley about a new season, she laughed and shut down the idea immediately.

"Girl, I'm already tired. This has already been a lot."

— Miley Cyrus, red carpet interview, March 2026

The anniversary special premieredTo be shown or performed in public for the very first time. on Disney+ on March 24, 2026. Whether you watched it as a child or discovered it later, one thing is clear: Hannah Montana was not just a TV show. It was a moment in pop culture that a whole generation shared together. And thanks to the power of nostalgiaA warm, happy feeling when you think about things from the past., that moment is alive again.

For Miley, this celebration is not just about the past. It is about accepting every version of yourself.

"I'd rather be like a gorgeous patchwork blanket. I'd rather take every little piece of all the things that I've been and create a mosaic of exactly who I am now."

— Miley Cyrus, Variety, March 2026

That, perhaps, is Hannah Montana's greatest legacySomething that stays important or remembered long after it happened. of all — not the wigs, not the songs, but the reminder that you don't have to choose between who you were and who you are becoming.
