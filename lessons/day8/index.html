She's Back —
And So Is Miranda Priestly

It has been nearly twenty years. Twenty years since Andy Sachs threw her phone into a fountain in Paris. Twenty years since Miranda Priestly looked out of a car window and smiled — that cold, terrifying smile — and drove away. And yet, somehow, the world never stopped talking about them. Now, in 2026, they are back. And the fashion world has never been more ready.

The Devil Wears Prada 2 is one of the most anticipatedExpected and waited for with excitement — usually refers to something people are very eager to see. films of the year. The original, released in 2006, became a cultural phenomenon — a film that was not just about fashion, but about ambition, identity, and the cost of success. Now the sequelA film, book or series that continues the story of an earlier one. arrives on May 1, 2026, with the same director, the same writer, and — most importantly — the same four actors who made the original unforgettable.

The story picks up nearly two decades later. Miranda Priestly is still editor-in-chief of Runway magazine — but the world has changed dramatically around her. Print media is in declineThe process of becoming smaller, weaker or less important over time.. Advertisers are leaving. Digital media has taken over. And Miranda, one of the most powerful women in fashion, is now fighting to keep her magazine — and her legacySomething that a person leaves behind that continues to have an impact after they are gone. — alive.

Andy Sachs, meanwhile, has become a respected journalist. She is recruited to return to Runway as the new features editor — which means working with Miranda again. And Emily Charlton, Miranda's former first assistant, has reinventedChanged completely to become something new and different. herself as a powerful executive at a luxury fashion group. The tension between the three women drives the entire film.

The film also introduces an impressive group of new characters. Kenneth Branagh plays Miranda's husband. Simone Ashley, Lucy Liu, Justin Theroux and Pauline Chalamet join the castThe group of actors who appear in a film, TV show or play.. And Lady Gaga — who has an original song in the film called "Runway", recorded with rapper Doechii — also makes a cameoMakes a brief appearance in a film or show, usually as a surprise..

By the numbers
  • The first teaser trailer broke records with 181.5 million views in its first 24 hours
  • The official trailer launched on February 1, 2026, got 222 million views in one day — the most successful trailer debut ever
  • Filming took place in New York and Milan, from June to October 2025
  • The working title during filming was "Cerulean" — a nod to the iconic blue sweater monologue from the original
  • The world premiere was held on April 20, 2026 at Lincoln Center, New York

What makes this sequel particularly interesting is its premiseThe central idea or situation that a story is based on.. The original film was set in a world where print magazines were powerful and glamorous. Today, that world barely exists. By setting the sequel in the age of digital media, social media and streaming, the filmmakers are asking a question that feels very real: what happens to powerful institutions — and powerful people — when the world changes faster than they can?

It is a question that goes far beyond fashion. And that is exactly why, nearly twenty years later, Miranda Priestly still feels relevantDirectly connected to and important in the present moment..
