Preview de ejercicios

Probá cómo se siente una lección real

Warm Up · Ejemplo real
Check your 'Screen Time' on your phone. Which app do you use the most? Does that app make you feel happy or stressed? Write about 50 words in English.
0 / 50 words
Content · Video + Vocabulario
Mirá el video y prestá atención al vocabulario clave del día.
📺 Video: How to use your phone as a tool, not a distraction
🛣️ Lead to — To result in something or cause something to happen.
🔔 Goes off — When a device like an alarm starts making noise.
🛠️ Coping skills — Techniques you use to manage stress or difficult emotions.
🌊 Overwhelmed — Feeling like a situation is too much to handle.
+ 6 palabras más incluidas en la lección completa
Deep Dive · La tarea principal
Dos actividades para profundizar lo que aprendiste hoy:
A)
Write your personal definition of Coping Skills. Give a few specific examples of yours.
B)
Build your Distraction Folder: list 1 song, 1 photo, and 1 funny video link that make you feel better.
Vocabulary Check · Fill in the blanks
Completá las oraciones con la palabra correcta.
Scrolling for hours can ______ anxiety and poor sleep.
I felt completely ______ by all the notifications.
Seleccioná la palabra correcta para cada oración
Checkpoint · Comprehension Check
According to the speaker, how can a smartphone become a tool for focus rather than a distraction?
¡Correcto! Usar el modo avión y un temporizador convierte el teléfono en una herramienta de foco.
Casi. La respuesta correcta es B — modo avión + temporizador es la estrategia sugerida.
